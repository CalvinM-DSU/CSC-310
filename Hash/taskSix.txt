Chaining had stable performance across the datasets and seems to be the most reliable,
    was even comparable to the std::unordered_map

Linear probing kind of broke down when faced with high collisions

Quadratic probing was more stable than Linear, though still not as great as Chaining

Double Hashing was again better than Linear, though worse than Quadratic

Chaining was hardly affected by high levels of collision. Quadratic probing and double
    hashing were slightly affected, but linear probing was heavily impacted.
